    History of TechCon®

    Origins (1996)

    - Inaugural Event: The TechCon® North America conference had its humble beginnings in 1996 in New Orleans, Louisiana. It was organized as part of the UK Radio Festival, under The Radio Academy.
    - Early Years: During its initial years, it served as a platform for industry experts to discuss technical development and solutions related to high voltage maintenance programs and asset management strategies for the aging electrical grid infrastructure.

    Growth and Independence

    - Moving Across the U.S.: As the conference gained popularity, it expanded its reach. TechCon® moved to different cities across the United States, accommodating an increasing number of delegates and utility hosts.
    - Independence: Since 2016, TechCon® has been run independently by TBC Media Ltd. This move allowed the conference to focus on its core mission and tailor its content to the evolving needs of the industry.

    Present Day

    - Annual Gathering: TechCon® North America remains an annual event, eagerly anticipated by utility professionals, electrical engineers, maintenance managers, and industry decision-makers.
    - Education and Networking: Attendees engage with global subject matter experts through technical white papers, panels, training seminars, tutorials, keynotes, and plenary addresses.
    - Expo and Connections: The conference features an exposition where industry suppliers showcase their latest technologies. It's a hub for networking, learning, and sharing knowledge.

    TechCon® 2025

    - Mark Your Calendar: The next TechCon® North America is scheduled for March 3–5, 2025, in Atlanta, GA.
    - Badges & Beer: Kick off the conference with the "Badges & Beer" Regi-ception on March 3, where you'll mingle with experts and fellow attendees.
    - Diverse Audience: Whether you're a newcomer or a seasoned professional, TechCon® welcomes generation, transmission, and distribution executives, engineers, security experts, and more.
